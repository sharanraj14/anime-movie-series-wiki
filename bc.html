Asta

Appearance

Asta is a young man of short stature with an exceptionally muscular build. He has green eyes and messy, ash-blond hair with bangs in front of his forehead and a single strand protruding upwards from the center of his head...

Personality

Asta generally is cheerful and hyperactive. He tends to shout to express his thoughts and goals, much to the annoyance of some of those around him, and often in spite of other people's opinions. One of the first examples of this is when he conveyed his goal of becoming a Wizard King during the Magic Knights entrance exam...

Yuno

Appearance

Yuno is a young man of average height with messy black hair, gold eyes and a lean build. He is often described and seen by others as a "handsome and good-looking guy" with Asta nicknaming him "pretty jerk" and once called so by Yami Sukehiro...

Personality

Yuno has a calm and emotionless demeanor and speaks only when he really needs to convey his thoughts. His manner of speaking often makes people misinterpret what he means. Despite his detached nature, Yuno harbors a strong ambition to be the Wizard King...

Black Bulls

Overview

Like the other eight squads, Black Bull follows the orders of the Wizard King and will act according to his requests. All squads, including the Black Bull, perform missions all around the Clover Kingdom whether they are official or unofficial...

Members

Black Bull is led by Yami Sukehiro, a captain who has the duty to delegate missions, relay messages received from the Wizard King, and distribute the members' salaries. Additionally, the captain decides whether a mage is to be accepted into the ranks of the squad...
